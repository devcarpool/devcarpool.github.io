Information We Collect

a. Personal Data

We may collect personal details such as your name, email address, phone number, and payment information when you create an account or use the app.

b. Biometric Data (Fingerprint and Face ID)

To verify participation in real carpooling trips and ensure accurate carbon abatement tracking, the app may collect biometric identifiers, including fingerprint scans and facial recognition data. This information is used solely for validating user presence during trips and is stored securely in compliance with biometric data laws.

c. Location Data

We gather your location in both foreground and background modes to:

  • Notify you when your ride is approaching.
  • Track trip routes accurately.
  • Match users based on proximity and destination.

d. Usage Patterns

We collect information on how you use the app, including ride history, preferences, and feedback.

e. Device Details

Your device type, operating system, unique identifiers, and mobile network information may be automatically logged.

f. Log Data

Standard server logs such as IP addresses, timestamps, and device actions are recorded during app usage.

How We Use Your Data

  • Service Delivery: To match users, coordinate rides, process payments, and improve the app experience.
  • Biometric Verification: To confirm actual ride participation and support validated carbon offset reporting.
  • Communication: To provide account updates, service alerts, and promotional content.
  • Security: To monitor for fraudulent or unauthorized behavior and verify identity.
  • Analytics: To track usage trends and improve functionality through anonymized data.

Sharing Your Information

  • Service Providers: Third parties may access your information to perform services (e.g., hosting, payment processing), under confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Compliance: Information may be disclosed to comply with laws or government requests.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a company restructure, your data may be part of the transferred assets.

Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined or as legally required. Biometric data is stored securely and retained only for the period needed to verify real ride activity and carbon offset validation.

Security

We apply robust technical and organizational security measures. However, no digital system is completely immune to security risks.

Breach Notification

If a data breach occurs involving your personal or biometric information, we will notify you promptly, including details and recommended steps.

Your Rights

a. Access and Correction

You may view and edit your information directly in the app.

b. Account Deletion

Users can delete their account from within the app. A prompt will warn that personal data and any earned rewards will be erased permanently.

To redeem points before deletion, contact support at support@carpool.com.au.

After deletion, all personally identifiable data will be erased, and trip-related data may be anonymized for operational and environmental reporting.

c. Biometric Opt-Out

Users may choose not to use biometric features; however, this may limit participation in verified carpooling and related carbon offset programs.

d. Retained Data

Some anonymized records (e.g., trip logs, payment history) may be retained for legal, operational, or audit purposes.

e. Marketing

You can unsubscribe from marketing messages via your account settings or opt-out links in communications.

Children's Privacy

CarPool is not designed for use by anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If we discover such data, we will delete it immediately.

Policy Updates

We may update this policy as our practices or legal obligations evolve. Major changes will be communicated through the app or our website.
